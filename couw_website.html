About Us

The Canadian Organization of Ukrainian Workers (COUW) is a non-profit organization dedicated to supporting and protecting the rights and well-being of Ukrainian workers in Canada. We aim to assist in their cultural, social, and economic integration while preserving Ukrainian heritage.

Goals & Objectives

  • Support the rights and well-being of Ukrainian workers in Canada.
  • Provide legal and advisory assistance on labor law, workplace rights, and social protection.
  • Organize educational programs, including language training and professional development.
  • Promote Ukrainian culture and identity through cultural events and initiatives.
  • Collaborate with organizations to improve living and working conditions for Ukrainian workers.

Membership

Anyone of Ukrainian descent or those interested in supporting the rights of Ukrainian workers in Canada can join. Members enjoy the right to participate in meetings, vote on organizational matters, and attend programs and events.

Responsibilities include adherence to bylaws, participation in organizational goals, and regular membership contributions.

Organizational Structure

Our governance includes a General Meeting of members, held annually, and a Board of Directors consisting of a Chairperson, Vice-Chairperson, and Managing Director. The Board is responsible for daily operations and implementing decisions from the General Meeting.
